Enables or disables the output of internal reference voltage (V\-R\-E\-F\-I\-N\-T) on I/\-O pin. The V\-R\-E\-F\-I\-N\-T output can be routed to any I/\-O in group 3\-: 


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item For Cat.\-1 and Cat.\-2 devices\-: C\-H8 (P\-B0) or C\-H9 (P\-B1).
\item For Cat.\-3 devices\-: C\-H8 (P\-B0), C\-H9 (P\-B1) or C\-H0b (P\-B2).
\item For Cat.\-4 and Cat.\-5 devices\-: C\-H8 (P\-B0), C\-H9 (P\-B1), C\-H0b (P\-B2), C\-H1b (P\-F11) or C\-H2b (P\-F12).
